About Us

DOMINE ICT, established in 1996, is a top-tier ICT consulting, training, and software solutions provider in Nigeria. We have partnered with organizations like the United Nations, state governments, and private enterprises to deliver world-class services.

Our journey began in 1994 with computer services and training, evolving into full-scale software development, consulting, and data analytics. Over the years, we have deployed high-quality solutions to federal agencies, NGOs, and educational institutions.
